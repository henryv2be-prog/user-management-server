Enhanced Door Icon Status Guide

Open Door (Yellow Neon Glow): Door is physically open - shows rotated door panel with yellow neon glow effect

Locked Door (Green Neon Glow): Door is closed and locked - shows lock icon with bright green neon glow

Unlocked Door (Red Neon Glow): Door is closed but unlocked - shows unlock icon with red neon glow warning

Offline Door (No Glow): Door controller is offline - muted gray appearance with subtle pulsing animation
