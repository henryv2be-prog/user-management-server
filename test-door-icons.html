Door Status Icons Test

Demonstration of the new door icons with status-specific glow effects

Icon Status Guide

Open Door (Yellow Glow): Door is open and unlocked - indicates potential security risk

Locked Door (Green Glow): Door is closed and locked - secure state

Unlocked Door (Red Glow): Door is closed but unlocked - security warning

Offline Door (Gray): Door controller is offline - no status available
